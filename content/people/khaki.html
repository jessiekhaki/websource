I am a second year PhD student in Statistics and Epidemiology in the CHICAS research group at Lancaster University. My research focuses on modelling spatial point pattern process data with applications to diseases such as typhoid. My work, which is funded by the Commonwealth Scholarship Commission, is being supervised by Dr Emanuele Giorgi, Dr Chris Jewell, Dr Marc Henrion, Professor Mavuto Mukaka and Professor Melita Gordon.

Previously, I obtained a Master of Science in Statistics at Lancaster University, a Master of Biostatistics (Chancellor College, University of Malawi) and a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and Mathematics (Chancellor College).
