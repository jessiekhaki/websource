I am a second year PhD student in the CHICAS research group at Lancaster University. I am studying towards a PhD in Statistics and Epidemiology. My research focuses on modelling spatial point pattern process and geostatistical data. Some of my work is applied to infectious diseases such as typhoid. My studies are funded by the Commonwealth Scholarship Commission.

Previously, I attained a Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics and Mathematics at Chancellor College, University of Malawi. I also hold a Master of Science in Biostatistics obtained at UNIMA and a Master of Science in Statistics from Lancaster University.
